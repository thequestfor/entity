# Entity Visual Mockup

Standalone mockup for Entity's visual presence.

Open `index.html` directly in a browser. No server, dependencies, or build
step are required.

The mockup explores a Soft Aero Cybercore direction:

- central glass presence
- emulated aero-glass blob material
- physical glass body with a base, meniscus, and soft support volume
- living 3D Frutiger Aero cybercore control room with perspective walls,
  glass side panels, console surfaces, light rails, slow refracted lanes, and
  atmospheric motion
- pale daylight blue, cyan, silver, and restrained graphite shell
- yellow appears only as a small semantic accent
- restrained CRT scanlines
- broken rim light, soft specular highlights, moving internal shadow pockets,
  background bubbles, frosted sparkle texture, and subtle refraction bands
- state-driven movement, including voice energy moving through the body in
  speaking mode as refractive threads, descending caustic rings, and fine
  light flecks, radial glass streaks, and active internal liquid light
- grounded shadow model with contact shadow, soft cast shadow, and reflected
  light under the glass body
- photorealistic glass cues through edge occlusion, lower-body shadow, firmer
  color shifts, and refractive internal flow
- softened action/service indicators that read as glass sheen rather than a
  tactical overlay
- small warm/green/red accents only for meaning

The control strip at the bottom lets you preview the states that Entity will
eventually drive from runtime state:

- idle
- listening
- thinking
- speaking
- acting
- autonomous goal
- waiting for confirmation
- service issue
- offline
